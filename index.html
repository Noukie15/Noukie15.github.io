About the Coins

ThomCoin

TommieToken

There are two types of shopping cart coins designed by Thomas: ThomCoin and TommieToken. They're 3D-printed, fit perfectly in shopping carts, and each has a unique message on the back. They're not legal tender — but they're worth their weight in gold when you're missing that one coin.

The Four Texts

"Unlock Your Cart"

This is the original coin. The key to shopping happiness. No more lugging around your shopping basket like a nomad — this coin unlocks not just carts, but a new era of convenience. Some say carrying this coin makes you 30% more efficient at shopping. Or at least you'll never have to ask: "Anyone got a coin?"

"Not for crypto, just for carts"

Not a digital currency, but with real value (especially at the supermarket). You can't trade this token on an exchange, and it's not accepted by Elon Musk – but pop it in a shopping cart and watch the magic happen. In a world full of NFTs and DOGE, this is simply a PMC: a Physical Money for Carts.

"Return me"

This coin is homesick. Maybe you found it on the street. Or in a parking lot. It probably rolled out of someone's pocket or was adopted by a random cart user. If you don't need it, pass it on. Or return it to Thomas (who's probably run back to the store 3 times looking for it).

"Property of Thomas"

This coin belongs to Thomas. But don't worry: using it won't get you arrested. It's more symbolic – a little piece of art in circulation, sponsored by Thomas himself. Feel free to use it, admire it, photograph it, or return it. (Or all four.)
